What is Password Keeper ?

Password Keeper is a desktop application made in the sole purpose of making your life easier when managing your passwords.

Everyone knows that having weak passwords is bad as it allows anybody to break it and access your personal datas. Though, it is even more tedious to remember each one of them especially when you created an account on a douzen of websites.

That's why Password Keeper has been developed by offering you an user friendly interface to stock and retrieve all your passwords. Now you can kep all of them in one place !
